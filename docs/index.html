How to reuse

  1. Place your study_area.shp (+ sidecars) and presence.csv under /data.
  2. Add GeoTIFFs to /data/rasters (any number, any names).
  3. Adjust parameters in the Rmd YAML (EPSG, background size, output names) and run the Rmd in your R environment.
  4. Outputs (e.g., suitability GeoTIFF and quick map) are written locally under /outputs.
